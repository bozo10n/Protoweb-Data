WWII in the Pacific (Iwo Jima, Pearl Harbour)



Description:
Various Quicktime videos of original WW2 news reels regarding the war in the Pacific:

Video #1 is a U.S. news reel showing U.S. Marines capturing Iwo Jima, a Japanese island used as a military base in World War II.

Video #2 reports on the Japanese attack on the American naval base at Pearl Harbor, Hawaii, on December 7, 1941.

I'd like to add more perspectives to it, so if you've got new reels from other countries about these events, please consider uploading them.
