Apple Music Store



Description:
Apple used to be the R&D company for the computer industry, spearheading a lot of developments. One field they came very late to the game was Digital Music though. The store started only back in October 2005 - more than half a decade after we all built up our mp3 collections using Napster. This video from Apple introduced the new store.
