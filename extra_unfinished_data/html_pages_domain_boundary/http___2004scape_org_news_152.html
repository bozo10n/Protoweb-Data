4th May 2024 - New World Map

Today we have released a superb replacement for our existing world map in the form of an applet. With the new applet you can scroll around and zoom in and out with ease. Saving the best to last, clicking items in the key highlights their placement on the map - helping you locate all sorts of useful places much faster.

... at least, that's what they said 20 years ago. The applet has been deobfuscated, refactored, and ported to modern browsers!
It also shows NPC and item spawns ;)
