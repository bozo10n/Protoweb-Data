Last night we had a major wind storm in the Pacific Northwest and we lost power for 6 hours, which was great compared to what others in the area are still going through. But that was no match for the small APC UPS which was holding up the PCjr and the networking gear. After an hour without power I shut the PCjr down to keep the networking gear up longer.

The PCjr had over 1400 hours of continuous runtime on it, and this time it was running without a hard drive. All of the web pages were being served from a network attached hard drive image served by a Raspberry Pi Zero 2W. I think I can declare the mTCP NetDrive code to be stable. ;-)
