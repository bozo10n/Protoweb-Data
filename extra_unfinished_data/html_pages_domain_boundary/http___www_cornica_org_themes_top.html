Themes on Cornica

Cornica offers you two different themes:

Classic: Uses a table layout, HTML 3.2 specs and runs fine on a 68k Macs. This remains our standard.

Advanced: Somewhat recreates the early 2000's apple.com look but without fluff. Good for users with G4 and G5 PPC Macs and browsers supporting CSS 2.1.

How does this work?

Scroll to the footer of the page where you get to choose which version you'd like to use. If you choose the advanced theme a cookie will be placed on your computer. The cookie simply stores info about your chosen theme and will last for 30 days, after which you'll see the standard theme ("Classic") again.

It doesn't work for me!

Please make sure your browser accepts cookies. You usually find that option within your browser settings. If it still won't display the theme, make sure you have CSS activated.

Do you plan to bring more themes to Cornica?

As of now I do not have any plans to add more themes, however, the option remains on the table. If you've got a great idea for a theme that would enhance the Cornica experience, please drop me a line.
