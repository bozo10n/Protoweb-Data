A/UX 1.0



Description:
A/UX ("Apple Unix") was Apple's attempt at marrying Unix with the Macintosh's GUI. Version 1.0 was tailored for the super expensive Mac II and released already back in 1988. This Computer Chronicles report is the earliest one I could find on it.

Interesting to think what could have been ihad Apple shifted its focus on A/UX development, possibly adopting it as its main OS!
