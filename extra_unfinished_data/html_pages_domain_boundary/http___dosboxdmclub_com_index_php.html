Like classic PC games, but want to play multiplayer the way it was originally intended?

The DOSBox Deathmatch Club community has got you covered. Join in our Discord community primarily focused on playing MS-DOS Multiplayer games via Classic Gaming Arena every Friday and Saturday at 8pm CST / 9pm EST! We also play classic Windows games (on Fridays only!) up to 2003 with some exceptions.

Not only we play the original versions of games, we aim to be more welcoming and have a casual-friendly environment. So come in, attend some events and have a fun time.

NOTE : When you join this community, you must verify either by using your email address or to introduce yourself in the #verification-chat text channel. This is to combat spam bots and the like.
