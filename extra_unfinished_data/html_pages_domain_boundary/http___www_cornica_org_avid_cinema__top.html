Avid Cinema



Description:
Back when iMovie came with the iMac G3 and started the whole digital home video craze for the masses, Avid Cinema had been there before. Pioneering the way that average home users could easily edit their movies through a friendly interface.

Years ago, Nathan Powers set out to change that by making this wonderful short film about Avid Cinema and the pioneer that has since been forgotten. Highly recommended!
